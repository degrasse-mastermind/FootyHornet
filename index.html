How it works.

Set the player, tap the play, share the live view, and review the full game story after the whistle.

01

Choose the player

Pick the approved player and team you are tracking. Parent Trackers only see the player/team contexts they have been approved to access.

02

Tap the play

Log goals, assists, shots, ball recoveries, clearances, saves, turnovers, tackle wons, 50/50s, hustle plays, smart plays, penalties, and notes with quick sideline buttons.

03

Share the live view

Send a read-only link so family can follow the timeline, score context, and player impact without editing the game log.

04

Review the impact

After the game, see totals, timeline, Game Impact, possession value, takeaways, tags, and season trends.

Team rosters stay protected.

Parents request access with a team code and jersey number. Admins approve the request. Once approved, the parent only sees the player and team they are allowed to track — not the full roster.
