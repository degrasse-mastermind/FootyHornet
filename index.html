Track the pieces that decide a soccer game.

Most stat apps stop at goals. FootyHornet is built around the full game story: who created possessions, who protected the ball, who handled pressure, and what changed quarter by quarter.

Scoring Goals, assists, shots, shot quality, and points

Keep production visible without losing the context around how chances were created.

Possession Ball recoveries, clearances, pressing, turnovers, and extra possessions

Capture the hidden work that changes momentum and gives the offense more chances.

Defense Caused turnovers, takeaways, pressure plays, and recovery notes

Show defensive impact with more detail than a basic box score can carry.

Specialists Saves, goalkeeper outlets, 50/50 wins, losses, and wing support

Give goalkeeper and wide and 50/50 work its own space instead of burying it in general notes.

Development Hustle plays, smart plays, penalties, tags, and coachable moments

Turn observations from a fast sideline into review points after the game.

Season Review Game Impact, timelines, trends, and shareable summaries

Review each game on its own, then compare the player's growth across the season.

How it works.

Set the player, tap the play, review the full game story, and share the recap after the whistle.

01

Choose the player

Add one or more local players on this phone, then switch to the correct player before each game.

02

Tap the play

Log goals, assists, shots, ball recoveries, clearances, saves, turnovers, tackle wons, 50/50s, hustle plays, smart plays, penalties, and notes with quick sideline buttons.

03

Share the recap

Use the game review and family recap to summarize the timeline, score context, and player impact after the final whistle.

04

Review the impact

After the game, see totals, timeline, Game Impact, possession value, takeaways, tags, and season trends.
