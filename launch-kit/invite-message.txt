Subject: Get started with FootyHornet

Hi everyone,

We are using FootyHornet to help track youth soccer stats this season.

Open the app here:
https://footyhornet.mybranford.com/

Parent setup:
1. Create an account with your email and password.
2. Enter your name, team code, and your child's jersey number.
3. Wait for admin approval.
4. Sign back in and choose your verified player/team tile.

Team code: ____________________

iPhone install:
1. Open the link in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
4. Open FootyHornet from the new icon.

FootyHornet tracks goals, assists, shots, saves, 50/50s, ball recoveries, clearances, turnovers, penalties, Game Impact, Average Impact, Effort Score, and season totals. Live Share also lets family watch updates from another iPhone, phone, tablet, or computer.
